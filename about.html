Our Story

Bringing Joy, One Stitch at a Time

Founded in 2026, Audrey&Co began with a simple mission: to create the world's most huggable companions. Every toy in our collection is designed with love and crafted from the highest quality, sustainable materials.

We believe that a plushie is more than just a toy—it's a friend for life, a source of comfort, and a keepsake that carries memories. Our team of designers works tirelessly to bring whimsical characters to life, ensuring each one has its own unique personality.

Whether you're looking for the perfect gift for a newborn or a quirky addition to your own collection, Audrey&Co is here to deliver magic and softness to your home.
